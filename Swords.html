Swords

Home Race awakenings Swords Fruits/Powers
  1. Swords are a weapon classification of up-close-and-personal, melee weapons that can be found and bought everywhere in the Blox Fruits universe. Most of these weapons have a focus on precise, close ranged moves. Swords are also one of the four main ways to deal damage in the game, along with Blox Fruits, Fighting Styles, and Guns.
